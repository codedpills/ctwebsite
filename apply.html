Program Structure and Duration

Our program stucture focusses on transforming people into professional app developers. Our students first go through 6 months practical skills training where they are taught how to code apps from scratch. After that high performing students will be selected and taken through another scholarship based 6 month Mentorship program where they work on real client projects to boost their portfolio and then we match them to internships and job opportunities.

PHASE I: 6 Months Practical Skills Training

Throughout the 6 months, students will learn how to code web and mobile apps from scratch with tools like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, React, Angular, Python, Node, PHP, Laravel, Java, Android, Swift, iOS, Git etc.

Courses: Web development, Android development, iOS development.

Duration: 6 months intensive

Fees: GHS 3,600

PHASE II: 6 Months Mentorship

Selected students who completed the training successfully will go through another 6 months Mentorship where they work on real client projects and get help from industry experts. This will enable them to boost their portfolios and become job ready.

Duration: 6 months

Fees: Merit based scholarship

Competent students who have participated in our Mentorship program will be matched to internships and job opportunities from our partner companies around the world.
